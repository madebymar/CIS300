"Snow Time"

This photoshoot was done in the small suburb of Ferndale, Michigan during a beautiful snowy afternoon. It had started snowing and I saw an opportunity of the challenge of shooting in the snow. A challenge it was indeed, not only because it was freezing outside, but also because the snow would try and get on the lens since it was also windy. I captured some really authentic moments playing in the snow, and close ups of both Jacob Knittel, Cosmo (the pup) and even myself. I used a Sony a6500 camera with a 16-70mm ZEISS lens and edited them in Lightroom and Photoshop. Special thanks to Jacob Knittel and Cosmo for enduring the super cold weather and being a part of this shoot. I am really happy with how they turned out, and would glady shoot in the snow again.
